Fire Watch Integration

üî• What is Fire Watch?

Fire Watch is a temporary measure intended to ensure continuous and systematic surveillance of a building or portion thereof by one or more qualified individuals or standby personnel.

Per Denton Fire Guidelines:

  • ‚Ä¢ Required when a fire protection system is out of service
  • ‚Ä¢ Fire Prevention Division must be notified immediately
  • ‚Ä¢ Patrols shall not exceed 60 minutes apart
  • ‚Ä¢ Fire Watch shall ONLY be terminated by the Fire Code Official

üìù In the Survey Form

When submitting a shutoff report, check the "Fire Watch Issued" box if applicable. This:

  • ‚úì Shows üî• icon in the incident log
  • ‚úì Helps track how long fire watch has been in place
  • ‚úì Includes status in the email to Fire Prevention
